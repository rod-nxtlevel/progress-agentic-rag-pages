The Problem

Your firm has terabytes of institutional knowledge. Case files going back decades. Contract templates. Regulatory filings. Court opinions. Internal memos.

Finding what you need means knowing exactly where it's stored, what it's called, and who worked on it. New associates spend their first year learning the filing system, not the law.

When a client calls with an urgent question, the answer is somewhere in your document management system. But "somewhere" isn't good enough when the clock is billing at $500/hour.

The Vision

Ask your entire document library a question

A senior partner asks:

"Find every contract clause related to force majeure that we've used in the past 3 years, and show me how courts in the Southern District have interpreted similar language."

Within seconds, Agentic RAG surfaces 14 relevant contracts, extracts the specific clauses, cross-references them with 3 court opinions from your research files, and presents a synthesized answer with full citations and links to the source documents.

No paralegal had to pull the files. No associate had to spend a weekend on research. The knowledge was always there — now it's accessible.

How It Works

From document chaos to legal intelligence

01

Ingest your document universe

Connect your document management system, case files, and research repositories. Agentic RAG processes PDFs, Word documents, scanned images (OCR), emails, and even video depositions. Automatic data anonymization ensures PII is handled — GDPR and privacy compliance built in.

02

Deploy secure search

Your attorneys get a search assistant that understands legal language and context. Ask questions in natural language across your entire document library. Every answer includes traceable citations back to the original source — no hallucinations, no guessing.

03

Accelerate every matter

Contract review. Due diligence. Regulatory compliance research. Case precedent discovery. What used to take days of associate time now takes minutes. Works across languages — critical for international firms.

04

Build institutional intelligence

Named entity recognition automatically tags every person, organization, jurisdiction, and date. Your firm builds a knowledge graph that makes every new matter faster because it connects to everything that came before.
